The brief

A vintage discovery app needed a campaign that made secondhand feel like a genuine style choice, not a compromise.

Good Fashion Friend is a mobile app that connects people with vintage finds from local stores in the Netherlands. I supported the team with customer research and a content campaign designed to grow app discovery and drive website traffic during a period when in person shopping wasn't possible.

02 Approach

01

Customer research

Ran an online survey to understand what customers actually wanted from the app. Findings pointed to demand for a wider range of vintage products and more options within the platform.

02

Audience profiling

Used the research to identify key social media influencers and build customer profiles that shaped who the content and campaign should speak to.

03

Campaign and content

During lockdown, developed a still life photography concept styling vintage pieces in a contemporary way, in collaboration with photographer Irmela Schwengler, alongside email and Instagram content to drive app discovery.

What the work delivered

Customer research that directly shaped content direction and influencer outreach

A distinct visual identity for the brand built around still life and styling, not studio fashion photography

04 Key takeaway

Vintage doesn't need to apologise for itself. Styling secondhand pieces with the same care as new collections is what made the campaign feel aspirational rather than like a compromise.
