The brief

A new skincare brand needed an identity, a positioning, and a reason to exist in a saturated market.

Onóre is a D2C mineral skincare brand built around the anti-stress properties of magnesium. I joined at the very beginning and worked across the full spectrum of brand building from market research and competitive analysis to brand identity, style guides, content strategy, and influencer collaboration. The challenge was finding a distinctive positioning in a crowded skincare market and making it stick across every touchpoint.

02 Approach

01

Research and positioning

Conducted in-depth market research using surveys, interviews, and competitor analysis. Identified magnesium as a unique differentiator an anti-stress ingredient with strong scientific credibility and high consumer relevance.

02

Brand identity

Collaborated with the graphic designer to build the brand identity from scratch developing 4 style guides and 3 brand guidelines that defined visual language, tone of voice, and product communication across all channels.

03

Content and influencer

Built and executed the Instagram content strategy, managed an influencer collaboration, and supported the brand's presence at the Green Beauty Expo bringing the brand into the market with a consistent and considered identity.

03 Execution

Instagram content strategy, influencer collaboration, and brand identity system developed across the launch period

Product photography from the brand shoot used across social and D2C channels

What the work delivered

A complete brand identity built from zero visual language, tone of voice, and product communication consistent across all touchpoints

4 style guides and 3 brand guidelines that gave the team a clear framework to build from

Brand positioning anchored in a genuine consumer insight 79% of urban youth experiencing stress that made magnesium a compelling and defensible differentiator

Successful launch at the Green Beauty Expo, influencer activation, and a live D2C brand in the CIS market

04 Key takeaway

The best brand positioning isn't invented it's found. Onóre's "MINERAL. SCIENCE. ANTI-STRESS." direction came directly from what the research told us about how people were actually feeling. Starting with insight meant every creative decision had a reason behind it.
